About Us

Established in 2024, Sky Pretoria is your premier aviation service provider located in the heart of Pretoria. Our mission is to elevate your aviation experience through unparalleled services, expert training, and breathtaking aerial tours.

At Sky Pretoria, we prioritize safety, professionalism, and customer satisfaction. Our team comprises experienced pilots and certified instructors who are passionate about aviation and dedicated to providing the highest standards of training and service.

Our Services

  • Flight Training: Comprehensive training programs for aspiring pilots.
  • Aerial Tours: Scenic flights showcasing the beauty of Pretoria and surrounding areas.
  • Fleet for hire: We hire out different aircraft fleet for special occasions.

Our Vision

To be a leader in the aviation industry by offering innovative solutions and exceptional service to our clients.

Our Values

  • Integrity: We adhere to the highest ethical standards in all our operations.
  • Excellence: Committed to delivering superior service and training.
  • Safety: Our top priority in every aspect of our operations.
