Marakele National Park

Located in Limpopo Province, Marakele is renowned for its breathtaking scenery and diverse wildlife. It's part of the Waterberg Biosphere Reserve and home to the Big Five, making it a nature lover's paradise.

Kwandwe Private Game Reserve

Located in the Eastern Cape, this luxury reserve offers exclusive safari experiences. Known for its cheetah conservation efforts and exceptional wildlife spotting opportunities.

Augrabies Falls National Park

Located in the Northern Cape, the park is famous for the roaring Augrabies Falls, known as "The Place of Great Noise" by the Khoisan people. It features stunning landscapes and rich biodiversity.

Nambiti Private Game Reserve

Located in KwaZulu-Natal, this reserve boasts a diverse range of wildlife, including the Big Five. It's also close to historic battlefields, offering a blend of adventure and history.

Tswalu Kalahari Reserve

Located in the Northern Cape, Tswalu is South Africa's largest private game reserve. It's known for its conservation efforts and unique desert wildlife, including pangolins and meerkats.

Golden Gate Highlands National Park

Located in the Free State, this park is famous for its golden sandstone cliffs and unique biodiversity. It's a popular destination for hiking, birdwatching, and exploring Basotho cultural heritage.

Royal National Park

Located in KwaZulu-Natal, this park is part of the Drakensberg Mountains and is a UNESCO World Heritage Site. It offers stunning landscapes and rare species like the bearded vulture.

Welgevonden Game Reserve

Located in Limpopo Province, this tranquil reserve is known for its commitment to conservation and its malaria-free status, making it ideal for families.

Kololo Game Reserve

Located in Limpopo Province, Kololo offers a unique blend of luxury and adventure. It's adjacent to the Big Five Welgevonden Game Reserve, making it an excellent base for self-drive safaris.
